# **7. AUTOPSY REASONING FRAMEWORK**

1. **Scene / Clinical Info**  
2. **External exam** — injuries, artifacts, premortem medical devices  
3. **Internal exam** — organ systems, distribution of pathology  
4. **Histology** — confirmatory sampling  
5. **Toxicology** — if indicated  
6. **Synthesis** — natural vs injury vs mixed  
7. **Cause mechanism → underlying cause → manner**  
8. **Report generation**  

**Pitfalls:**  
- Mistaking postmortem artifact for injury  
- Mislabeling manner of death  
- Over-reliance on single toxicology result  
- Under-sampling critical organs  
- Confusing mechanism with cause  
- Missing medical devices or surgical complications
